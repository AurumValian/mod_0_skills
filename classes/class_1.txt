MenuItem class
Attributes:

name (string)
gluten-free (boolean)
vegan (boolean)
price (float)
servings (integer)

Methods:

add_croutons() (if gluten-free was true, changes gluten-free to false)
double_serving() (increases serving size, increases price)
change_price() (increases or decreases price)
change_name("string") (changes name of the item)
